## 🎯 Key Features Added

### ✅ Complete Nutritional Data
Every ingredient includes:
- Calories, protein, fat, carbs
- Fiber, sugar, sodium
- Water content & water activity
- Density for accurate measurements

### ✅ Proper Categorization
- `fruit` - Fresh, dried, candied, jams, jellies
- `nut` - All nut varieties
- `spice` - Ground spices and blends
- `fat` - Cheese-based fats (cream cheese, mascarpone)
- `other` - Extracts, candy, seeds, mix-ins, specialty

### ✅ Real Science Data
- Water Activity (aw) values for shelf stability
- Density values for volume-to-weight conversions
- Accurate nutrition facts for FDA compliance
